Understanding Age Pension: Your Guide to a Secure Retirement

The Age Pension is a vital financial support system for retirees in Australia. It helps ensure a comfortable standard of living during retirement, but eligibility criteria and benefits can vary.

How Does Age Pension Work?

  • Government Support – Provides financial assistance to eligible retirees.
  • Income & Asset Test – Determines the amount of pension you receive.
  • Regular Payments – Offers consistent income to help manage living expenses.
